Discipline & Environment

Hostel Rules & Safety

Safety Features

  • CCTV Surveillance: 24/7 camera monitoring in all common areas and corridors.
  • Biometric/Gated Entry: Controlled access to ensure unauthorized persons cannot enter the premises.
  • Resident Warden: A supportive warden is always on-site to handle any emergencies.
  • Fire Safety: Equipped with fire extinguishers on every floor.

Code of Conduct

  • Quiet Hours: Strict silence maintained after 10 PM to facilitate undisturbed study.
  • Gate Timings: The main gates close at 10:00 PM. Late entry requires prior permission.
  • Substance Free: Strict no-smoking and no-alcohol policy within the hostel premises.
  • Guest Policy: Outsiders/friends are not allowed inside the rooms to maintain privacy and security.
