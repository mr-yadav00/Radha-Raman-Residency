About the Residency

Built for Focused Minds

Radha Raman Residency is a thoughtfully designed student accommodation located in Sector-5, Talwandi — one of Kota's most accessible student neighbourhoods. We understand what it takes to perform under pressure, and we've built a space that supports that journey.

From warm, well-furnished rooms to a supportive warden who goes the extra mile, every aspect of the residency is designed to let you focus on what matters most — your studies and your future.
